Privacy Policy

CROOM X (package: pal.vishal.cromx)

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CROOM X (“we”, “us”, “our”, or the “App”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our Android application. By using CROOM X, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

CROOM X is a casual multiplayer games app. It uses virtual points only. Virtual points have no real-world cash value and cannot be withdrawn, sold, or exchanged for money.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & profile information

If you sign in with Google, we may collect:

  • Google account identifier and authentication tokens (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Display name and email address associated with your Google account
  • Profile photo you choose (camera or gallery) or an in-app avatar
  • User ID used to identify your account in our services

Guest / skip-login mode may allow limited use without a full account.

2.2 Gameplay & app activity

  • Game scores, ranks, leaderboard data, and match history
  • Virtual points balance and Virtual Room entry amounts
  • Task / daily reward progress and login streak status
  • Online room codes and match participation data

2.3 Device & technical information

  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Network status (to enable online play)
  • Advertising ID (AD_ID), used by advertising partners where permitted
  • Crash / diagnostic information needed to keep the App stable

2.4 Permissions we may request

  • Internet / network — online play, sign-in, ads, and cloud sync
  • Camera & photos — optional profile picture only
  • Notifications — task reminders and login-streak reminders (optional)
  • Vibrate / wake lock — gameplay feedback and reliable reminders

We do not require access to your contacts, SMS, microphone, or precise location.

3. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage your account and profile
  • Provide multiplayer games, Virtual Rooms, leaderboards, and rewards
  • Track virtual points and fair gameplay outcomes
  • Show rewarded advertisements
  • Send optional reminders (for example, daily tasks or login rewards)
  • Improve app performance, security, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests and enforce our Terms
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database / Firestore, Storage) — accounts, game data, profile images
  • Google Sign-In — account authentication
  • Google AdMob — rewarded ads and related advertising identifiers
  • Google Play services — platform features required by the App

These providers have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob & privacy.

5. Advertising

CROOM X may show rewarded ads through Google AdMob. Ads may use your device’s advertising identifier and similar technologies to deliver and measure ads. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (for example, “Delete advertising ID” or equivalent options on Android).

If your device supports it, you can reset or delete your advertising identifier in Android settings.

6. Virtual Points

Virtual points are in-app units used for gameplay features such as Virtual Rooms. They are not money, not a stored-value account, and have no cash value. We do not sell virtual points for real money withdrawal, and you cannot redeem them for cash, goods, or services outside the App.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With service providers listed above, to operate the App
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect rights, safety, and integrity of users and the App
  • With your consent, or as otherwise disclosed at collection

Some profile information (such as display name, avatar/photo, score, and rank) may be visible to other players on leaderboards and in online matches.

8. Data Retention

We retain account and gameplay data while your account is active and as needed to provide the App. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize associated personal data from our active systems, except where we must retain limited information for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

9. Data Safety (Google Play)

For Google Play “Data safety” disclosures, the App may collect and process:

  • Account info (Google Sign-In): email and basic profile details
  • App activity: gameplay, scores, leaderboards, Virtual Room participation
  • Photos: profile photo only (if you choose to upload one)
  • Device or other IDs: advertising ID (AD_ID) for ads

Data is used to provide core app features (online play, profiles, leaderboards), to operate ads, and to improve stability and security.

10. Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the App: Profile → Delete Account.

Deletion permanently removes your profile, scores, ranks, profile photos, and related account data from our services, subject to the retention limits above. Google Sign-In users may be asked to re-authenticate before deletion.

11. Children’s Privacy

CROOM X is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Because the App includes ads and online multiplayer features, it is intended for a general audience and is not designed as a children’s app under Google Play Families policies.

12. Security
