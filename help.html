Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order?
Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our products, select the item you want, choose size/color, and click "Buy Now" or "Add to Cart". Then fill in your delivery details and confirm your order. No account required!
Do I need to create an account?
No, you don't! We offer Guest Checkout. You can place your order without creating an account. Just enter your name, phone number, and delivery address.
What payment methods do you accept?
We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD). You pay when you receive your order. No online payment required!
How long does delivery take?
We deliver within 3-7 business days depending on your location. You'll receive a confirmation message once your order is shipped.
Is delivery free?
Yes! We offer free delivery on all orders above Rs.499. For orders below Rs.499, a small delivery fee of Rs.49 applies.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day easy return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 7 days of delivery. Visit our Returns Policy page for more details.
How can I track my order?
You'll receive an order confirmation with an Order ID. You can use this ID to track your order status. We'll also send you updates via message.
What if I receive a damaged product?
Don't worry! If you receive a damaged or defective product, contact us immediately. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund. Please take photos of the damaged item for faster resolution.
